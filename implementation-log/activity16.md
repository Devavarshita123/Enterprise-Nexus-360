Employee Notification

## Objective
Notify employees automatically when their onboarding process begins.

## Business Requirement
Employees should receive an email confirmation after onboarding approval without requiring manual communication from HR.

## Flow Enhancement
Added an email notification action at the end of the onboarding workflow.

## Email Content
- Confirmation of onboarding approval
- Provisioning has started
- Employee will receive further updates

## Concepts Learned
- Flow Designer Notification Actions
- Automated Communication
- Event-driven Processes

## Outcome
Implemented automatic employee notifications, improving communication and transparency during onboarding.
